Is ImageMojo Pro completely free? expand_more
Yes! All our tools are 100% free to use for standard tasks. We do not require a credit card. However, we have usage limits for unregistered users. Creating a free account gives you higher file size limits and faster processing speeds.
How do I compress a PDF file? expand_more
1. Go to the PDF Tools category.
2. Select "Compress PDF".
3. Upload your file and choose your compression level (Low, Medium, High).
4. Click "Compress" and wait for the download link.
Are my files secure? expand_more
Security is our top priority. All files uploaded to ImageMojo Pro are processed securely and deleted from our servers automatically after 2 hours. We do not look at, copy, or share your files with anyone.
Why did my upload fail? expand_more
Uploads usually fail due to:
  • Unstable internet connection.
  • The file exceeds the size limit (50MB for guests).
  • Unsupported file format.
If the problem persists, please try a different browser or clear your cache.
